Return, Refund & Terms

Return Policy
  • Return vehicles by the agreed date and time at the specified location.
  • Return the vehicle in the same condition as rented, excluding normal wear and tear.
  • Late returns incur extra charges per the hourly/daily rate.
  • Excessive dirt may result in a cleaning fee.
  • Refill fuel to the original level, or refueling charges will apply.
  • Damage, missing parts, or loss will be charged as per actual repair or replacement cost.
  • Inspection with our representative is recommended at return.
Refund Policy
  • Full refund for cancellations at least 48 hours before pickup.
  • 25% fee for cancellations within 48 hours of pickup.
  • No refunds for cancellations after scheduled pickup or no-shows.
  • Modifications subject to price and availability.
  • If the vehicle is unavailable, a full refund or replacement will be offered.
  • Refunds processed within 7–10 business days to the original payment method.
For any questions or special cases, please contact our support team.
